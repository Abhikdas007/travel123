about us

ROAD LINK TOURISM is Indian Travel Company founded in the year 2019. Headquartered in Siliguri, West Bengal. The company provides online travel services including domestic and international holiday packages, hotel reservations especially in Darjeeling, Sikkim, Kalimpong, Dooars, Bhutan & nearby.

Since we opened our doors, we have dedicated ourselves to planning trips that provide our clients with beautiful memories for years to come. So whatever type of trip you’re dreaming of, we’ve got you covered. Get in touch today and we’ll take care of the rest.
